About

Dohyeon (Danny) Kim is a software engineer at Coupang. He has completed Master's degree at the Department of Industrial and Management Engineering at POSTECH (Pohang University of Science and Technology) in South Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Minseok Song. His research interests center on software engineering, process mining, data mining, and data visualization. He was the member of Analytics & Information Management Lab. (Prof. Minseok Song) from 2016 to 2018
